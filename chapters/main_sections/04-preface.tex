This report is written by group 5.215b at the faculty of engineering and science at Aalborg University. 
The theme is \textit{statistical modelling and analysis}, and has the purpose of giving a fundamental introduction to statistical modelling for economic applications. 

The group would like to give a special thank to: \\
- \textit{Jakob Guddal Rasmussen, lecturer in Statistics, for introducing and clarifying results related to the course Statistical Inference for Linear Models.}\\
